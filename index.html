What We Do

Our Services

💍

Wedding Catering

We understand that a wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime event. Our wedding catering service is built around that emotion. From mehendi lunches to grand reception dinners, we handle multi-course menus, live counters, dessert spreads, and everything in between.

  • Custom bridal menu planning
  • Live chaat, dosa, or pasta counters
  • Full setup and service staff
  • Veg, Non-Veg, Jain options

⚠️ Inquiry only — contact us for custom pricing.

🏢

Corporate Catering

Professional, hygienic, and on-time. We cater to office meetings, annual events, product launches, and team celebrations. Our corporate menu is curated for taste and professionalism.

  • Packaged meal boxes or buffet setup
  • Teas, snacks, and lunch/dinner options
  • Flexible scheduling around your event
  • Bulk pricing for large teams

⚠️ Inquiry only — contact us for custom pricing.

🍛

Buffet Service

Our signature buffet is an experience in itself. With a spread of over 20 dishes served fresh and hot, this is ideal for large celebrations. Minimum 50 packs required.

  • Starters, mains, breads, rice, desserts
  • Full buffet table setup & service
  • Delivery + on-site service available
  • Priced per pack (minimum 50)

✅ Bookings accepted online!

🎉

Events Catering

From birthday parties and kitty parties to puja ceremonies, griha pravesh, and social functions — we cater to all personal celebrations with the same dedication we bring to grand events.

  • Customisable menus for all budgets
  • Delivery, pickup, or full setup service
  • Short lead time accepted
  • Veg, Non-Veg, sweet boxes available

✅ Bookings accepted online!
